Common questions

How do recommendations work?

Describe your taste (or snap a bottle) and PocketSom's AI sommelier curates wines matched to you. The more you heart, save, and add tasting notes, the sharper it gets.

How do I delete my account?

Open Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all your data.

Why does it ask for camera / microphone access?

The camera is used to photograph a bottle or a wine list; the microphone is for optional spoken tasting notes. Both are optional and only requested when you use those features.

The app needs the internet?

Yes — recommendations are generated by our backend, so an active connection is needed to curate new wines. Your saved library is available offline.

Please enjoy wine responsibly. Must be of legal drinking age.
