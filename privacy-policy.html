PocketSom Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PocketSom ("we," "us," "the app") is a wine-recommendation companion. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We do not sell your data, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Information we collect

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a user identifier and, where you permit it, your email address. We use this only to create and secure your account.
  • Your taste data. The wine descriptions you write, wines you heart or save, tasting notes, chat messages, and the taste profile we derive from them. This is the core of the app — it powers your recommendations.
  • Photos you provide. Bottle and wine-list photos you choose to capture or upload. These are sent to our AI provider to identify the wine or read the menu, and are not retained long-term on our servers after processing.
  • Voice input. If you use voice tasting notes or voice dictation, audio is transcribed to text. On supported devices this happens entirely on-device; on older devices it may use Apple's speech-recognition service. We store the resulting text, not the audio.

How we use it

  • To generate and personalize your wine recommendations.
  • To save your library (favorites, wishlist, tasting notes) and sync it across your devices.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the app.

We use your data only to provide app functionality. We do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Service providers

  • Supabase — hosts our database and authentication. Your account and taste data are stored here, protected by per-user access controls.
  • Anthropic — provides the AI (Claude) that generates recommendations and analyzes the photos you submit.
  • Apple & Google — provide sign-in.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy commitments.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can clear your wine data at any time in Settings → Reset wine data, and you can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data in Settings → Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your authentication record and your per-user data from our systems.

Children

PocketSom is intended for adults of legal drinking age and is not directed to children.
